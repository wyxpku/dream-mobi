HENAN LIUMANGGANG ELEMANTARY SCHOOL LIBRARY

Liumangang Elementary School library is located in Liumangang Elementary School Liumangang Village Xinghuaying Town Jinming District Kaifeng City Henan Provence and it was established in 2010 May. As one of the four libraries built by Dream Corps in Henan, Liumangang library consists of three class rooms, which are located in the second floor of the main building. The school accommodates 270 students and teachers, over 4000 books and 315 library users.

THE STORY OF THE LIBRARY

Beijing Zhufang Village New Century Dream Library is located in Beijing’s Haidian District, at 52 Zhufang Village Front Street. It was established by Dream Corps in 2006 and is still operating today. This community library is relatively mature in that it has completed its collaboration with the Summer Volunteer Program and currently receives help from long-term volunteers who organize reading activities every weekend on site. This library now owns 2,096 books and has received immense support from many charity organizations.

LIBRARIAN

YING WANG

Seventh and ninth Math Teacher

Mr. Wang currently serves as both Dream Corps’ contact person at Niujie and 1kg Books’ 1kg Ambassador. In his spare time he enjoys reading, watching movies, exercising, and listening to music. He firmly believes that reading not only can enrich students’ daily school life, but can also help underprivileged children to understand the world and get out of their existing environment.
